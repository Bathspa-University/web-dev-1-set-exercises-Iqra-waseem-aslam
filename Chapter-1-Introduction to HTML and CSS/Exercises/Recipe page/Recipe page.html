simple pancake Recipe

Welcome to my Recipe page, where culinary inspiration meets delicions creation!

Ready Time

  • Total:
  • prepare:20 minutes
  • cooking:6 minutes

ingredients

  • 200g Philadelphia light Cream Cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 230ml semi-skimmed milk
  • 200g flour
  • 8g bakery yeast
  • 1/2 tea spoon salt
  • 1 table spoon sugar
  • 200g blueberries

steps

  1. mix cream cheese and the egg until obtaining a homogeneous dough
  2. Add the milk and mix
  3. Add flour, yeast, salt, and sugar, and mix until obtaining a homogeneous dough
  4. let it rest one hour
  5. add the blueberries
  6. cooking in pan: drop a big spoon to form a pancake in the pancook it until bubbles are bursting on the top side,flip it cook the other side less time
  7. serving time: put in a serving plate and put vanilla ice cream on the top with some blueberries
  8. ENJOY WITH HOT COFFEE
