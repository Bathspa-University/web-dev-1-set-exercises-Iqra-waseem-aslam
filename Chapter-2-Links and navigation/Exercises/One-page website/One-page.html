Overview

Four English siblings were moved to a country homemade where they would be safe during the World War

II bombardment of London. When Lucy (Georgie Henley) discovers a wardrobe one day, it takes her to the fantastical land of Narnia.

She soon makes her way back to Narnia with her sister Susan (Anna Popplewell) and brothers Peter (William Moseley) and Edmund (Skandar Keynes).

There, they team up with Liam Neeson's mystical lion Aslan to battle Tilda Swinton's evil White Witch, Jadis.
