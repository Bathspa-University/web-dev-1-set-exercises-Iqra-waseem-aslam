Welcome to Digi Fest

Digi Fest is a celebration of digital and technology in the city of Cardiff. This year, we are celebrating ‘collaboration’, so come and find out about the latest collaborations that are happening in this historic and innovative city. We promise an educational and inspirational packed programme for all.
